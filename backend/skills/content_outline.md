# Content Outline Skill

## Purpose
Create an SEO-structured content outline that guides the writing agent.

## Structure Rules
- H1: Exactly one, contains primary keyword
- H2: 4-8 major sections covering full topic breadth
- H3: Subsections under H2s for depth
- FAQ: Minimum 5 questions targeting PAA and long-tail queries

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] At least 2 H2s contain primary or secondary keyword
- [ ] FAQ covers "People Also Ask" queries
- [ ] Content differentiators are specific, not generic
- [ ] Meta title under 60 characters
- [ ] Meta description 150-160 characters with keyword + CTA
